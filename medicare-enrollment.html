Automations We've Built
Real automations already running

These aren't slideware. Each of these is a working automation built for live Medicare telesales operations — designed to remove busywork, protect compliance, and let agents stay on the phone.

① Lead Intake & Routing

New leads are captured, de-duplicated, scored, and routed to the right agent automatically — no manual list-pulling or copy-paste between systems.

② Real-Time Agent Assist

AI listens to the conversation and surfaces the right plan details, rebuttals, and next questions on screen, live, without the prospect ever knowing.

③ Compliance Disclosure Checks

Required CMS disclosures, scope-of-appointment, and recording prompts are tracked in real time, flagging gaps before the call ends.

④ Auto Call Summaries → CRM

Every call is transcribed, summarized, and logged to the prospect's record with outcome, objections, and next steps — written for you.

⑤ Follow-Up & Reminder Engine

Callbacks, SEP/AEP deadlines, and follow-ups are scheduled automatically and pushed to agents so no enrollment window gets missed.

⑥ Manager Command Center

Live dashboards roll up pipeline, agent activity, compliance risk, and conversion so managers see status and act in one view.
