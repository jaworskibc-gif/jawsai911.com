## Core Offer

JAWSAI911 helps local businesses stop losing time and leads by building practical AI workflows:

- website/contact form cleanup
- missed-call and lead follow-up automation
- CRM/inbox organization
- quote/intake forms
- appointment reminders
- basic AI assistant/chatbot setup
- email/SMS follow-up scripts
- workflow review and action plan

## Positioning

Not "AI magic."

Say:

I look at where leads, calls, and follow-ups are leaking out of your business, then I build the simple AI/automation system to catch them.
